About DBest Kuantan

A congregation of friends in 1988 to meet the eminence of education. Most of the friends were diverse and from many backgrounds. These friends were committed and believed in the goal of achieving their objectives. Thus, born a group of friends whom lived, entertained and embracing the meaning of perpetual journey to the unknown and perilous time ahead.

Among the friends, maturity and intelligence enforcing them to accept everyone as an exceptional individuals. Soon, they understood that friendship could not be bought but always in existence for the years to come. Hence, the group of friends from the same program of Diploma of Business Studies for batch 1991 from UITM (University Teknologi MARA), Kuantan nee ITM was built from the ramification of such ardous experience. Enhancing this, DBest Kuantan was formed as a bridge to fill the interval and sharing of oneself and the whole.
